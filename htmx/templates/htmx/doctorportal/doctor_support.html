Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new test for a patient?

Navigate to Create Test in the top navigation bar. Fill in the patient's age and optionally their email address, then click Create. A unique test link will be generated that you can copy and send to the patient.

How long is a test link valid?

Test links expire 72 hours after creation by default. Tests that have not been started will show a status of Pending; once the patient begins the test it becomes Active.

Where can I view a patient's results?

Go to Results in the navigation bar. Find the test in the table and click View Results. Results are only available once the test status is Completed.

Can I download results as a file?

Yes. On the individual test result page, use the Download CSV button to export the patient's results as a spreadsheet-compatible file.

What does the "Tests Expiring Soon" section show?

The dashboard highlights any pending or active tests whose links will expire within the next 48 hours so you can follow up with patients in time.

How do I update my account email or password?

Go to Settings in the navigation bar to manage your account information.
